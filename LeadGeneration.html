Lead Generation.

To generate potential customers/clients through social media and search engines.

Lead generation is the process of attracting prospect buyers who are interested in your business that is product or service. A lead is someone intrested in your business so to make him into a potential customer it is important to approach him in the place at the right time.

Lead generation is a very important activity for your business as it helps the business in improving it's sales. It only makes sense to sell a particular product or service when the customer is interested if not there won't be any transaction between the buyer and the seller.

There many ways to generate leads from social media platforms or electronic media through email marketing, blogging, social media platforms, websites, etc. Lead generation ultimately will help you improve your sales of the business.
