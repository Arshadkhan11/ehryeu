Search Engine Optimization [SEO]

Increase the traffic and visibility to your site and help you with organic ranking.

Search Engine Optimization is a process of increasing a website's visibility and ranking. It is more focused on free or organic traffic to your site.

Website optimization is divided into three main factors.
* On page optimization
* Technical optimization(on sets)
* off-site optimization.

It is a process of optimizing the various elements of the pages throughout the site. It is focused on elements like content, keywords, and optimizing the page data.

Technical | On-site:
Unlike on-page optimization, it has more technical aspects to improve the website. It is focused on how well the site is seen and understood by the search engine.

Offsite optimization:
It is the process to improve the site visibility outside your website.
